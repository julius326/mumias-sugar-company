Our Products

Sugar Products

  • Brown Sugar: A less refined form of sugar that retains natural molasses, giving it a richer flavor and darker color. It is popular among households and bakeries for baking, sweetening beverages, and adding depth to recipes.
  • White Sugar: Fully refined sugar that is clear, sweet, and versatile. It is packaged in a wide range of options such as 1kg, 2kg, 5kg, and bulk 50kg bags to serve households, shops, supermarkets, and large-scale commercial buyers.
  • Fortified Sugar: Enriched with Vitamin A to help fight micronutrient deficiencies in Kenya. This sugar not only sweetens but also contributes to improved health, especially among children and vulnerable populations. It is distributed widely through supermarkets, retailers, and wholesale distributors.

Other Products

  • Ethanol: Produced mainly from molasses, Mumias Sugar Company manufactures ethanol for industrial use. It is supplied to industries in the beverage sector, pharmaceutical companies, and energy firms for blending with fuels.
  • Bottled Water ("Mumias Sprinkles"): Fresh, purified bottled water available in different volumes including 500ml, 1 liter, and 20 liter containers. It is sold to households, offices, hotels, and for events, offering safe and clean hydration.
  • Electricity: Generated from bagasse (the fibrous residue of sugarcane), Mumias Sugar Company produces 34 Megawatts of power. A portion is used within the factory to support operations, while surplus electricity is sold to the national grid, contributing to Kenya’s renewable energy supply.
  • Molasses: A thick syrup obtained during sugar processing. It is supplied to farmers as animal feed supplement, to industries for alcohol production, and to traders for resale. Molasses is also a raw material for ethanol and other chemical processes.
